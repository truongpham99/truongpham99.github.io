Research

Theoretical Analysis of Submodular Information Measures for Targeted Data Subset Selection
Nathan Beck*, Truong Pham*, Rishabh Iyer
arXiv, 2024

Submodular Mutual Information (SMI) has been applied to perform targeted subset selection with the aid of a exemplar query set. For the first time, we provide bounds on relevance and coverage of the targeted data in terms of the SMI objective value.
